About ComicFix

ComicFix is a premium team building platform specifically designed for developers participating in hackathons based in Kerala. We offer a range of cutting-edge features to help teams collaborate seamlessly and efficiently during the hackathon process.

Our platform simplifies the process of team formation, providing advanced collaboration tools such as real-time chat, file sharing, and project management features. Additionally, ComicFix connects participants with experienced mentors who provide valuable guidance and support throughout the hackathon journey.

At ComicFix, we are committed to empowering Kerala developers to unleash their creativity, collaborate effectively, and achieve outstanding results in hackathons.
